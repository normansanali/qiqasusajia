Cub Cadet 7305 Price, Reviews, Specs, Engine Oil Capacity, Weight, Serial Numbers,  Attachments, History, Features & Pictures

Cub Cadet 7305 Compact Utility Tractor is equipped with a Mitsubishi diesel 3-cylinder liquid-cooled 91.29 ci [1.5 L] engine, which delivers 30 hp [22.4 kW] power at rated rpm 2750.

Cub Cadet 7305 Compact Utility Tractor featured a Mitsubishi 1.5L 3-cyl diesel engine, 4×4 MFWD 4WD, power steering, 1,653 lbs [749 kg] Rear lift (at 24″/610mm), 2465 to 2685 pounds Weight, 5 qts [4.7 L] engine oil capacity, and infinite (3-range) forward and reverse gears.

Cub Cadet 7305 Compact Utility tractor attachments include a 60″ Mower deck, 72″ Mower deck, Cub Cadet 476 loader, and Cub Cadet 408 backhoe.
